About Zion Advisor

Zion Advisor is a boutique consulting practice focused on audit-ready databooks, financial due diligence support, infrastructure research, transaction advisory and investor-grade financial models. We blend senior review gates, repeatable processes and automation-first workflows to deliver consistent, defensible outputs on tight timelines.

Our approach

  • Deal-first: investor-grade reconciliations, QoE support and operating schedules tied to source data.
  • Research-led: sector intelligence and infrastructure analysis that improves transaction certainty.
  • Automation-native: AI-assisted parsing and reconciliation to shorten cycle time without losing review control.
  • Execution-minded: recommendations tied to budgets, timelines, governance and operating realities.

What we do

  • Audit-ready databooks, schedules & working papers
  • Financial due diligence, QoE and transaction support
  • Fundraising models, investor packs & scenario analysis
  • Infrastructure research, feasibility & market intelligence
  • PMO, governance and delivery frameworks for complex infrastructure projects
  • Decarbonization, procurement and net-zero transition advisory
