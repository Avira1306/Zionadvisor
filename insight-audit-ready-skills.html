Making Skilling Programs Audit-Ready (Without Slowing Delivery)

Published 2025 • 6 min read

1) Evidence Packs that Match Schemes

Map each deliverable to the precise scheme clause. Use file naming conventions and a centralized index for fast retrieval during audits.

2) Geo-Tag Rules & Attendance

Use standardized geo-tags and timestamping. Cross-check attendance logs with batches, trainers, and lab sessions.

3) Vendor & Equipment Documentation

Pre-approve vendor lists, maintain serials/warranty, and keep photo logs. This avoids last-mile documentation gaps.

4) MIS Cadence that Scales

Adopt a monthly/quarterly reporting rhythm with a simple dashboard, status flags, and reminders. Reduce end-of-quarter scramble.

  • Geo-tag & attendance checklist
